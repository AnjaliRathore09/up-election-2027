How to Vote: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Registration (Doing it NOW)

Ensure your name is in the Voter List. Visit nvsp.in to check or register.

Step 2: Locate Your Booth

Find your polling station using your EPIC number on the Voter Helpline app.

Step 3: Election Day

Carry a valid ID, go to your booth, and press the button on the EVM. Wait for the 'BEEP'!
